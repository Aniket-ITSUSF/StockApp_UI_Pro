Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AlphaDesk ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our paper trading and AI evaluation service.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data - pages visited, evaluations run, tickers searched, timestamps, device type, browser, and approximate location (country/region).
  • Account data (when accounts launch) - email, hashed password, subscription tier, billing identifiers from our payment processor.
  • Technical data - IP address, browser language, referral source, and cookies described below.

How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve the service, including AI agent evaluations and discovery.
  • To serve and measure advertisements (see Advertising below).
  • To detect abuse, fraud, and quota violations.
  • To communicate service updates and respond to support requests.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use first-party cookies for session state and preferences (e.g., market selection, dismissed banners). Third-party cookies are used by our advertising and analytics providers as described below. You can control cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our service. Google and its partners may use cookies and identifiers to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Measure the performance of advertisements.
  • Prevent fraudulent or invalid clicks.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or visit aboutads.info to opt out of third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising.

If you reject non-essential cookies via our consent banner, we will request that Google serve only non-personalized ads on this site.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense - advertising. Policy.
  • Yahoo Finance - market data provider; we do not share user data with them.
  • Hosting providers - Railway and similar infrastructure for service operation.

Data Retention

We retain usage logs for up to 12 months and account data for the duration of your account plus a reasonable period thereafter for legal and accounting requirements.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Children

The service is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent changes.
